Privacy Policy

Mood Meal - AI-Based Food Suggestion App

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Mood Meal ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our AI-based food suggestion mobile application ("the App").

Information We Collect

  • Mood Data: Information about your current mood, either by selecting predefined options (e.g., happy, sad, stressed) or providing free-text descriptions.
  • Usage Data: Information about your interaction with the App, including features accessed, food suggestions viewed, and session times.
  • Device Information: Includes device model, operating system, unique device identifiers (e.g., device ID, advertising ID), and mobile network details.
  • Optional Information (with consent):
    • Dietary Preferences and Restrictions: Allergies, dietary restrictions (e.g., vegetarian, vegan), and food preferences.
    • Location Data: If enabled, your location is used to offer nearby food suggestions. You can disable this in your device settings.

How We Use Your Information

  • Providing Food Suggestions: We use your mood and preferences to generate personalized meal recommendations using AI algorithms.
  • Improving the App: Usage data helps us enhance app functionality and add new features.
  • Personalization: Optional data enables more tailored experiences based on dietary needs and location.
  • Communication: If you provide contact details, we may send updates or promotions. You can opt-out at any time.
  • Research and Development: Aggregated and anonymized data may be used to improve our AI and user experience.

Sharing Your Information

  • Service Providers: We may share information with third-party vendors who support app functionality (e.g., hosting, analytics). These parties are contractually bound to protect your data.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your data if required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • Business Transfers: In case of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred. We will notify you of any significant changes via the app or email.
  • With Your Consent: We will share your data with third parties only with your explicit consent.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to deliver app services, fulfill legal obligations, and resolve disputes. Mood data may be retained briefly to improve mood-food mapping. Anonymized, aggregated data may be retained indefinitely.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access personal data we hold about you
  • Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Object to data processing under certain circumstances
  • Request restrictions on how we process your data
  • Request data portability in a machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via the email provided in the App. We may ask for identity verification before fulfilling your request.

Security

We implement reasonable security measures such as encryption, secure storage, and routine assessments to safeguard your data. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe your child has provided personal data, please contact us, and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the App or other means. Continued use of the App after such updates signifies your acceptance. Please review the policy periodically.
